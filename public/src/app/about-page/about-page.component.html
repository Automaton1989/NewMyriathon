Gaming for Good

We're a group of friends who play games and give to charity

Myriathon was founded by Matt “abandon” Rossi in 2013 after being inspired by a number of online marathons and meeting with members of Team World Vision to support their efforts for clean water in third-world countries. Since then, the Myriathon team has expanded in both scope and size, continuing to work with new charities, welcoming new members to the community, and exploring new forms of media to foster an environment where everyone can share in fun activities during their big events and their off-season streams.
